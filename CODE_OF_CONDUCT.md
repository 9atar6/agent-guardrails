## Code of Conduct

Agent Guardrails is an open specification and toolchain for the AI coding community. We expect everyone participating in the project to treat each other with respect.

- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions.
- Assume good intent; disagree with ideas, not people.
- Do not tolerate harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Keep discussions focused on improving the spec, examples, and tooling.

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, you can contact the maintainer privately (see `SECURITY.md` for contact options) or open a confidential report via GitHub.

Maintainers may act to moderate discussions, edit or remove content, or restrict participation in order to keep the community safe and productive.
